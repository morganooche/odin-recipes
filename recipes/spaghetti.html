Quick Spaghetti Recipe

Description

This is the easiest spaghetti recipe that can be done in just under 15 minutes! Pair this spaghetti with a side salad or bread for a delicious meal. Recipe makes enough for 4 people and is perfect for a family dinner.

Ingredients

Boxed pasta:

  • one 12-ounce box Barilla® Pronto® Half-Cut Spaghetti
  • 4 quarts boiling water
  • 1 pinch of salt

For sauce:

  • one 24-ounce jar Barilla® Tomato and Basil Sauce or your favorite sauce
  • fresh basil
  • parmesean cheese

Steps

Cook the pasta:

  1. To a large pan, add the pasta, cover with 3 cups cold water, optional salt to taste, and boil over high heat until water has absorbed, about 10 minutes, but watch your pasta and cook as needed until al dente.
  2. After pasta has cooked for about 10 minutes, or until all the water has been absorbed.

Add the sauce:

  1. Add the sauce over the pasta and toss to combine in the skillet or alternatively plate the pasta and add sauce to each individual plate as desired.
  2. Sprinkle parmesean cheese over the pasta and enjoy!
