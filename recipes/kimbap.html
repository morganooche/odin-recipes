Homemade Kimbap Recipe

Description

A delicious seaweed rice roll filled with a delicious tuna mayo filling.

The main ingredients of tuna kimbap are canned tuna, mayonnaise and sesame oil.

This serves well as a snack or a light meal.

In the recipe below, I will show you how to make super quick and easy tuna kimbap

Ingredients

For rice:

  • 4 cups white rice
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vinegar

For tuna filling:

  • 1 can tuna
  • 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil

Steps

Prepare the rice:

  1. Put the freshly made steamy rice into a large bowl. Add salt and vinegar and gently fold it in with a rice scoop or a wooden spoon.
  2. Set aside and let the rice cool down.

Prepare the tuna filling:

  1. Heat a small pan over medium high heat. Add the tuna, mayonnaise, sesame oil.
  2. Stir the mixture for about 2 minutes with a wooden spoon until the tuna turns a little flaky. Remove from the heat.

Roll the gimbap:

  1. Divide the rice into 4 portions. Evenly spread 1 portion of rice over the gim.
  2. Divide the tuna mixture into 4 portions. Spread one portion across the middle of the rice, in a strip lengthwise.
  3. Use both hands to pick up the bottom edge of the mat. Use it to roll the gim up and over the fillings so the rice surrounds them.
  4. Squeeze the mat gently and then continue rolling the gim in the mat, squeezing the mat regularly to get a tight roll.
  5. Unroll the mat and remove the roll from the mat. Set the roll aside, with the seam down.
  6. Sprinkle the sesame seeds over top.
